ONE PIECE

If you love pirates and their adventure quests, then One Piece is one anime series you do not want to miss. Based on the story of Monkey Luffy, a 17-year-old adventurer and his friends who are in search of a secret treasure, “One Piece” is an epic story of resilience. The Grand Age Pirates aim to be the first-ever pirates to discover the One Piece and be proclaimed Pirate King. This is a prestigious title in the world of piracy at that age. This anime first aired in 1999, and since then, 800+ episodes have followed. Although it has changed rights and networks many times, the anime is still among the top anime of all time.
