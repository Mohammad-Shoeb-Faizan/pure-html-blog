Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer)

Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer) is a Japanese manga series by Koyoharu Gotōge. A popular manga about humans and demons, along with terrifying swordfights.First Launched in Feb 2016. It’s a Story-Based of a soft-hearted boy who sells charcoal for a living and finds his family slaughtered by a demon. And the Sole Survivor, his younger sister, Turned into a beast Herself. Till Now, Season 1 is Completed, and there is an announcement of the first Movie based on this Anime
