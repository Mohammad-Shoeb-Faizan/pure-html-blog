Bleach

Bleach is one of the classic anime still loved by new generation fans. The anime is about young Ichigo Kurosaki, a student who has always had the inexplicable ability to make contact with spirits. But his life changes when he meets a shinigami (Reaper of souls) during a battle with an evil entity called the Hollow. From there begins the multiple adventures for Ichigo Kurosaki.
