NARUTO SHIPPUDEN

A sequel to the original anime naruto that aired up to 2007, Naruto Shippuden continues with the story of Naruto Uzumaki in his quest to become a Hokage. This anime is set two years after Naruto has left Konohakagure to train as a ninja. There is an organization of elite Ninjas, Akatsu, which includes powerful Ninja-like Itachi Uchiha, that is planning on taking over the reign of the Shinobi world. Naruto has not changed that much in this anime compared to the first version. His confidence, however, has grown, and he fights with ore heart and resilience. This anime is made up of a massive 500 episodes that aired between 2007 and 2017. It ranks as one of the top 10 anime films of all time.
